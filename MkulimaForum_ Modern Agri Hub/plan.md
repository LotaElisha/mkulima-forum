# MkulimaForum — Software Architecture Plan

## Overview
Transform the existing Mkulima Super App architecture document into a comprehensive, modern MkulimaForum platform tailored for the East African agricultural ecosystem. Add new features: Agrodealer Marketplace, Plant Disease Scanner, Farmers Forum, and Services (Agronomist, Logistics & Transport, Warehouse, Veterinary Help, Soil Testing).

## Stage 1 — Research
- **Skill**: `deep-research-swarm`
- **Objective**: Gather intelligence on East African agritech landscape 2025-2026, mobile money infrastructure, crop disease patterns, regulatory frameworks (TFRA, KEPHIS, etc.), logistics providers, and existing platforms.
- **Agents**: 3 parallel research agents covering: (1) East African agritech & regulatory landscape, (2) Mobile money & fintech integration patterns, (3) Crop diseases, AI/ML for agriculture, and logistics infrastructure.

## Stage 2 — Architecture Document Writing
- **Skill**: `report-writing`
- **Objective**: Produce a comprehensive, chapter-by-chapter architecture document (15,000+ words)
- **Structure**:
  1. Executive Summary & Vision
  2. East African Context & Problem Statement
  3. Platform Overview — MkulimaForum Modules
  4. System Architecture (High-Level + Microservices)
  5. Laravel Backend Architecture (Domain-Driven + Modern)
  6. Database Architecture (PostgreSQL + Advanced Schema)
  7. AI/ML Integration (Plant Scanner, Soil Analysis, Recommendations)
  8. Flutter Frontend Architecture (Clean Architecture + Modern UI)
  9. Module Deep-Dives: Marketplace, Forum, Services, Disease Scanner
  10. API Design (RESTful + Standards)
  11. Payment & Financial Architecture
  12. Real-Time, Logistics & Maps
  13. Security, Compliance & Data Sovereignty
  14. DevOps, Deployment & Scaling
  15. Development Roadmap & Milestones
  16. Risk Analysis & Mitigation
  17. Cost Estimation & Resource Planning
- **Agents**: Sequential chapter writing with parallel review agents.

## Stage 3 — DOCX Formatting
- **Skill**: `docx`
- **Objective**: Convert final markdown to professional .docx document.

## Deliverable
- `/mnt/agents/output/mkulima_forum_architecture.docx` — Professional, comprehensive software architecture document.
